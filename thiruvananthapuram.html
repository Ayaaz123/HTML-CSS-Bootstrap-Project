About Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital of Kerala. It is known for its beaches, historical monuments, and vibrant cultural scene. The iconic Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kovalam Beach, and the Napier Museum are among its popular attractions.
